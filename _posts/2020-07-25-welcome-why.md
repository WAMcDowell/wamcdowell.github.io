*A very brief explanation of the purpose of this blog.*

![](/images/2020-07-25.png)

I believe anything you intend to invest a significant amount of your time should be accompanied with a clear understanding of “Why.” In the case of this blog the purpose is to chronicle the things I learn about various topics of interests. Selfishly I intend it to be a reinforcing mechanism to improve my retention of knowledge. If I happen to gain an audience so be it and may you find some benefit from reading my ramblings.
